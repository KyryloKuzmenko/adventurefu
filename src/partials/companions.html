Unlock Powerful Companions and Power-ups

  • Companions

    Unlock various animal companions that aid you on your journey. Each pet has unique abilities that make gameplay more exciting.

  • Power-ups

    Collect power-ups during gameplay to gain temporary boosts. These include:

    • Speed Boost — dash through levels at lightning speed.
    • Invincibility Shield — become immune to enemy attacks for a short period.
    • Double Score — earn double points for every action you complete.
